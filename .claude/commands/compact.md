# /compact

*Pratyāhāra — withdrawal. Know when to let go.*

Compact when:
- Context window reaches ~50% (do not wait for 80%+)
- A major task has been completed
- The conversation has shifted topic significantly

When compacting:
1. Summarize what was accomplished
2. List decisions made and why
3. Note what remains pending
4. Preserve any critical gotchas or blockers discovered

The goal is not to summarize everything — it is to preserve what the next session needs to know to continue well.

> *"Vairāgyam"* — Non-attachment. Release what is no longer needed.
