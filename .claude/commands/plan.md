# /plan

*Viveka — discernment before action.*

Before executing any non-trivial task, produce a plan:

1. **Restate the goal** in one sentence — confirm you understand what's being asked.
2. **Identify unknowns** — what information is missing or ambiguous?
3. **List the steps** — ordered, concrete, specific.
4. **Flag risks** — what could go wrong? What is irreversible?
5. **Confirm before proceeding** — wait for explicit approval unless the task is clearly low-risk.

Do not start executing while planning. Planning and doing are different modes.

> *"Ā-rabhate 'lpam"* — Begin with the small. (Yoga Sūtra principle)
